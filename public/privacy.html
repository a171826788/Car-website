At ATAT, we are committed to providing the highest standard of luxury transportation. That commitment extends to how we protect the data of our valued clients.

We safeguard your personal information with the utmost discretion and responsibility, utilizing the most advanced security protocols to ensure your privacy is never compromised during your journey.

The Details

TRANSPARENCY IN HOW WE PROTECT YOUR DATA

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us when registering on the site, expressing an interest in obtaining information about us, or when booking our premium services. This includes your name, contact details, payment information, and specific travel preferences.

Your data allows us to curate a seamless and bespoke travel experience. We use this information to manage reservations, process secure transactions, accommodate your personalized requests, and improve our overall service quality.

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze site traffic, and personalize content. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings at any time.

You have the right to access, rectify, or erase your personal data. You may also restrict processing or request data portability. To exercise these rights, simply contact our Data Protection Officer.

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

As a global service provider, your information may be transferred to and maintained on servers located outside of your jurisdiction. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for all international data transfers.
